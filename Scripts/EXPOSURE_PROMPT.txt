Plaintiff sustained a displaced fracture at the radial base of the proximal phalanx

C. Lost Wages
Plaintiff contends that the use of his left hand is critical to his ability to perform

Plaintiff will argue that despite being right-handed, the mechanics of card dealing

Based on the wage records produced by Plaintiff, Plaintiff is expected to earn
